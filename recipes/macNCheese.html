Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This recipe is the best in the world. I found in hidden in the basement in Italy

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 4 ounces cubed processed cheese food
  • 2 fluid ounces milk
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.

  2. Place a saucepan over medium-low heat. Combine the cheese food, milk, and pepper in the saucepan;

    cook until the cheese has melted, stirring frequently. Stir in the drained macaroni until evenly coated.
